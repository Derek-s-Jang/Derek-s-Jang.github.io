About Me


Hey everyone! My name is Derek Jang and I am an incoming fourth year Computer Science Major, Statistics Minor, and Sociology Minor in University of Toronto. I am from a family of five including myself, my twin brother Kyle, my parents, and last but not least, my golden retriever Blitz.

During my time in university, I have learned many programming languages such as Java, Python, and C. Building the many applications and projects in class taught me more than just having code that works, as the professors also emphasize code optimization and architecture in our work. The school projects additionally inspired me to create my own personal projects and learn Javascript by myself. Aside from school though, my favorite activities are working out and playing video games (yes, I do realize that they seem to contradict each other). I have always had a interest in computers ever since getting into PC gaming at a young age, and I always keep up to date with the latest and greatest AAA games! I also really enjoy working out as it is a way for me to relieve stress while staying active and healthy.
